# Peripheral mapping of Pi

## Screen mapping (Pi - Peripheral)

5V - VCC
Ground - GND
GPIO18 - LED
GPIO23 - RESET
GPIO24 - D/C
GPIO8 - CS
GPIO11 - SCK
GPIO9 - SDO(MISO)
GPIO10 - SDO(MOSI)

## Keyboard mapping
